## üìå Project Overview
This project synthesizes **129,880+ global passenger records** into a strategic "Command Centre" to diagnose brand health and operational efficiency. The goal is to identify the mathematical **"Breaking Point"** of service delivery, evaluate the **"Frustration Carry-over"** effect, and isolate high-impact opportunities for **revenue recovery and service revitalization**.

**Key metrics analyzed:**
- **Overall Satisfaction Rate (%)**  
- **30-Minute Operational Redflag (Delay Impact)**
- **Service Influence Gap (Satisfied vs. Dissatisfied)**
- **Revenue per Available Seat Kilometer (RASK)**

## üîç Key Analyses
- **Sentiment Decay Mapping:** Identified the 30-minute threshold where passenger trust and repeat-booking intent collapse.
- **Service Influence Analysis:** Ranked 14 service dimensions (WiFi, Booking, Gate, etc.) to distinguish between "Basic Needs" and "Loyalty Drivers."
- **Customer Segmentation:** Evaluated satisfaction gaps across **Business vs. Economy** and **Loyal vs. First-time** travelers.
- **Operational Friction Audit:** Isolated the "Bottom 5" departments causing the highest negative impact on brand equity.

## üõ† Tools & Skills
- **Power BI:** Advanced Data Modeling, Star Schema, Slicer Synchronization.
- **Power Query:** "Magic Unpivot" logic, Text Scrubbing, Hybrid Date Reconciliation.
- **DAX:** Calculated Measures for Satisfaction Decay, Weighted CSAT, and Percentage Variances.
- **Analytical Strategy:** Root Cause Analysis, Sentiment Psychology, Revenue Architecture.

## üìà Key Insights
- **The 30-Minute Breaking Point:** Beyond 30 minutes of departure delay, satisfaction scores crash by 25%, indicating a critical operational limit.
- **Ground-to-Cabin Friction:** Poor digital experiences (Online Booking/WiFi) create a "negative lens" that diminishes the perceived value of the actual flight.
- **Economy Emergency:** Identified an **81.54% dissatisfaction rate** in the Economy segment, signaling a massive risk to the airline's highest-volume revenue base.

## üîç Detailed Strategic Insights

### **The "Frustration Carry-over" Effect**
- Data confirms that a poor digital experience (Online Booking/WiFi) acts as a **"negative lens."** Passengers frustrated at the start of the journey are significantly less likely to rate in-flight catering or seat comfort highly, regardless of actual quality.

### **The 30-Minute Operational Redflag**
- Analysis of 130k records mathematically isolates **30 minutes** as the "Breaking Point." Beyond this mark, the probability of a passenger remaining "Satisfied" drops to nearly zero, regardless of seating class.

### **Economy Class Emergency**
- With **8 out of 10 Economy passengers** reporting dissatisfaction, the airline‚Äôs largest revenue base is at high risk. The data suggests that "Ground-to-Cabin" friction is the primary driver, rather than the flight itself.
